How It Works

Step 1: Create Your Queue
  • Sign up or log in to your account.
  • Click on the "Create Queue" button.
  • Enter a unique name for your queue and set any desired preferences or instructions.
  • Save your queue, and it's ready to go!
Step 2: Share Your Queue
  • Once your queue is created, you'll receive a unique URL and a QR code.
  • Share the URL or display the QR code at your service location.
  • Customers or participants can easily join the queue by scanning the QR code or visiting the provided URL.
Step 3: Have Customers Join the Queue
  • Customers or participants access the queue by clicking on the URL or scanning the QR code.
  • They enter their relevant details, such as name or contact information.
  • After joining the queue, they receive a confirmation with their position in the queue.
Step 4: Manage Your Queue
  • As the queue owner, you have a dedicated page to manage your queue.
  • Monitor the list of attendees in real-time, seeing their names and positions.
  • Attendees who have been served can be removed with a simple click of the "X" button.
  • The queue automatically updates and displays the new positions for the remaining attendees.
Step 4: Real-Time Updates
  • Attendees can check their current position in the queue by refreshing the provided URL.
  • The system dynamically updates their position, keeping them informed about their wait time.
